Home

We are the Teen Alliance on Mental Health, We built this site for teens, adults, etc either stuggling with mental health or struggling to support someone struggling with mental health issues. We hope to provide insight from our perspectives to help each other and help break down the stigma around mental health. We have articles relevant to what is going on around us today, and references to support sites. In the future we want to continue to branch out our site and make it as inclusive as possible.
